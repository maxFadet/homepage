How to Make Classic Cheesecake

  1. Mix the crushed cookies with the butter.
  2. Press it into the bottom of the mold and place it in the refrigerator.
  3. Mix the cheese with eggs, sugar, and salt.
  4. Add sour cream, heavy cream, vanilla, lemon juice, cornstarch, and mix well.
  5. Pour the prepared mixture into the mold, removing any bubbles.
  6. Pour a little water into a baking tray and place our mold in it.
  7. Place the baking tray with the cheesecake into a preheated oven at 180Â° for 30 min.
